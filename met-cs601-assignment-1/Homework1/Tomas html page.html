Jaromir Jagr

Who is Jaromir Jagr?

Jaromir Jagr is a professional ice-hockey player from the Czech Republic

Where is Jaromir Jagr from?

Jaromir Jagr was born 15th of February in Kladno, Czech Republic.

Who did Jaromir Jagr play for?

Jaromir Jagr played for Hc Kladno in Czech Republic. At the age of 18 he was drafted into the NHL, where he played for the Pittsburgh Penguins, Washington Capitals, New York Rangers, Dallas Stars, Boston Bruins, Florida Panthers and Calgary Flames.

How many points did Jaromir Jagr get in the NHL?

Jaromir Jagr has played 1733 games in the NHL. In those games he scored 766 goals and added 1155 assists collecting 1921 points.

How many times has Jaromir Jagr won the Stanley Cup?

Jaromir Jagr has won the Stanley Cup two times with the Pittsburgh Penguins.

Jaromir Jagr's team trophies

  • 2x Stanley Cup
  • 1x Olympic gold
  • 2x World Cup gold
  • 1x Olympic bronze
  • 2x World Cup bronze

Jaromir Jagr's individual trophies

  • 5x Art Ross trophy
  • 1x Hart trophy
  • 2x World Cup gold
  • 3x Ted Lindsay trophy
